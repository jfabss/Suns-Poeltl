SUNS POELTL

HOW TO PLAY:

Try and guess which Suns player is in the silhouette.

A green clue is an exact match

A yellow clue is a close match (within 2 years, inches, or numbers).

A white clue is no match.

You have 5 guesses to name the Sun.

(Roster Range: 2020-24)

Good Luck!!
